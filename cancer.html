What is Cancer



Normally, human cells grow and multiply to form new cells when the body needs them. This process is called cell division. When cells become old or damaged, they die and new cells take their place.

Cancer is a disease in which some of the cells in body grow uncontrollably and spread to other parts of the body. Cancer can start almost anywhere in the human body which is made up of trillions of cells grouped to form tissues and organs.
