What is Cancer

Cancer is a disease in which cells in the body grow out of control and spread to other parts of the body.

Cancer can occur in almost any part of the human body. The human body is made up of trillions of cells.

Normally, human cells grow and multiply to form new cells when the body needs them (a process called cell division). When cells become old or damaged, they die and new cells take their place.
